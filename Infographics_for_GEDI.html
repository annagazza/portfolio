NEWSPAPER DASHBOARDS

Infographics for Gedi

Exploring societal topics through printed dashboards

I designed a series of dashboards for La Repubblica’s monthly inserts to visually communicate topics such as health, environment, and technology. Each dashboard translated journalistic data into clear and readable visual narratives while respecting the distinct visual identities of the inserts. Working within tight production timelines required balancing clarity, consistency, and visual variety. The resulting dashboards offered readers concise overviews of diverse subjects, supporting understanding and engagement while maintaining the newspaper’s editorial and graphic standards.

ROLE

Independently designed dashboards based on data provided by journalists

SKILLS

Data visualization, graphic design, editorial design

15

La Repubblica is one of Italy's leading newspapers and belongs to GEDI Gruppo Editoriale, one of the most important Italian publishers operating across various sectors, including newspapers and magazines, radio, internet, and television. La Repubblica features monthly inserts on different topics characterised by a specific colour palette, layout, and fonts defined by the newspaper’s graphic team.

CHALLENGE

Designing dashboards within a tight weekly schedule required balancing clarity, usability, and adherence to the visual identity of La Repubblica’s monthly inserts.

.2

Starting from content provided by journalists (tables, statistics, reports, and text), I designed and laid out visualizations, following the inserts’ established style and guidelines.

Each dashboard was completed within a week, requiring careful organization to balance visual coherence with varied chart types. For Italian Tech Week 2021, the deadline was reduced to two days, demanding focused time management and adaptability.

[C] Draft of the dashboard I created for the Italian Tech Week 2021

SOLUTION

The final dashboards combined visual appeal with clarity, offering readers concise and informative insights across a variety of topics while maintaining consistency with the inserts’ visual identity.

RESULT

The dashboards enhanced visual clarity and appeal, helping readers engage with and understand a variety of topics. By aligning with the brand identity and incorporating diverse chart types, they delivered consistent and accessible insights.
