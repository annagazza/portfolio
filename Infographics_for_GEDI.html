NEWSPAPER DASHBOARDS

Infographics for Gedi

During my internship at Accurat, I independently created 15 dashboards for two different supplements of La Repubblica. Each dashboard consisted of a series of visualizations that explored a specific topic, with data provided by La Repubblica journalists. My task was to transform this data into clear and intuitive visualizations that aligned with the brand identity of the two supplements.

La Repubblica is one of Italy's leading newspapers and belongs to GEDI Gruppo Editoriale, one of the most important Italian publishers operating across various sectors, including newspapers and magazines, radio, internet, and television. La Repubblica features monthly inserts on different topics characterised by a specific colour palette, layout, and fonts defined by the newspaper’s graphic team.

GOAL

To create clear and intuitive dashboards within a tight weekly schedule, adhering to well-defined visual identities.

Each dashboard began with content provided by a journalist, which came in the form of tables, statistics, reports, and text. Following the visual style and guidelines of the inserts, I created and laid out the visualisations, ensuring visual coherence while varying the types of charts used.

Typically, each dashboard was completed within a week. However, for the Italian Tech Week 2021, I was required to deliver the final file in just two days. This situation demanded organisational and time management skills to meet the deadline.

[C] Draft of the dashboard I created for the Italian Tech Week 2021

OUTCOME

The final dashboards were visually compelling and provided readers with clear, concise, and informative insights on a variety of topics.

[D] The particular of a dashboard for “Green and Blue” about the environmental impact of the food chain

[E] Two dashboards I designed for “Salute”, the first one is about the benefits derived from vacations and the second is related to the infant mortality

[F] The dashboard I ideated for “Green & Blue” explores the topic of greenhouse gas and other polluting agents

RESULT

The dashboards improved visual appeal and clarity, boosting reader engagement and understanding of diverse topics. They aligned with brand identity, ensuring consistency while using a variety of chart styles.
