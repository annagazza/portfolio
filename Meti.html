WEB APP

Meti

Enhancing industrial processes with AI-driven guidance

I redesigned the brand identity and web app of Meti, a software that combines AI and statistics to enhance knowledge in production environments. The project positions the app as a “friendly guide” for operators: processes remain precise and reliable, while the interface becomes intuitive and accessible. The visual language draws on a constellation metaphor, where individual elements gain meaning through relationships, forming an evolving system that orients and supports users. By reorganizing content, clarifying terminology, and providing visual guidance, the redesign helps operators navigate tasks efficiently, while the new structure accommodates future growth and functionalities.

In production environments often characterized by fragmented information and undocumented solutions, Meti offers an alternative: a shared and accessible process built through collective contribution. Meti provides operators with tools to diagnose and resolve production issues through a knowledge-mapping platform and a web app for problem identification, guidelines, and technical documentation.

CHALLENGE

Content moderation on social media is a socially relevant and often opaque process. To shed light on how Facebook policy violations are addressed and communicated to the public, TTP created a dataset from news articles documenting policy violations on the platform between 2018 and 2021, and Meta’s responses. This dataset provided the foundation for a data-driven narrative.

I facilitated workshops with technical and commercial stakeholders to clarify the product’s tone, purpose, and positioning. The discussion highlighted a key positioning: Meti should act as a continuously evolving, supportive guide rather than a purely technical tool.
This insight inspired a visual metaphor based on constellations: elements that gain meaning through relationships, belong to an ever-evolving system, and historically served as tools for orientation and navigation.
The constellation metaphor guides the visual system: gradients suggest evolution, while lines and dots (taken from the logo) evoke connected elements. Explanatory micro-copy helps users navigate the app. The monospaced secondary font highlights AI-related and instructional content, reinforcing clarity and reliability.

[A] Some of the first sketches of the app

The original web app struggled with scalability and clarity: it lacked a home section, offered all content at once, and presented interactions without a clear hierarchy. These issues made the interface unintuitive and limited its ability to support future features.

[B] I restructured the troubleshooting section to introduce a responsive layout and a scalable structure capable of supporting multiple functionalities

The redesigned app introduces a clear and scalable structure that helps operators navigate tasks efficiently. A new home page organizes functions and provides a central point of orientation.
The troubleshooting chat now simulates a real conversation: selectable responses stay anchored at the bottom, and contextual actions appear directly on the relevant elements. Automated messages guide users step by step, introducing the logic of the chat in a tutorial-like manner and avoiding the artificial impression of user input before any action.

[C] I redesigned the detail view using a scrollable bottom sheet with accordions, allowing long texts to remain accessible without disrupting the chat flow

[D] I integrated feedback directly into the chat, keeping users aware of selected or excluded causes within the conversation context

[E] I clarified chat history by introducing a legend that makes previously opaque states understandable at a glance

Issue reporting has been streamlined by pre-grouping items under their main sections, reducing friction and guiding users through the workflow. These improvements make the app more intuitive and support effective task completion.

SOLUTION

The redesigned web app makes processes accessible without oversimplifying them. Operators can confidently navigate tasks, quickly access procedures and documentation, and rely on a system that adapts to their expertise level. The structure accommodates future functionalities, ensuring the platform evolves with user needs.

RESULT

Meti helps operators quickly access relevant procedures and information, with AI-driven suggestions supporting faster, more reliable decision-making. The flexible and scalable structure supports growth, and reinforces the brand’s promise of a “friendly guide” in industrial environments.
