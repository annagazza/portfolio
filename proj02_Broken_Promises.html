Broken Promises

Tech Transparency Project (TTP) is a research initiative of Campaign for Accountability, a nonprofit organization that exposes misconduct in public life, especially regarding the big tech companies. TTP contacted Accurat to create a digital experience aimed to expose Facebook’s policy violations that occurred between 2018 and 2021 and how the platform has reacted to them. Facebook spokespersons have continuously promised that they would take action, but from 2018 until now the number of annual violations is about the same. So we chose the glitch as a metaphor to symbolize this premise; the glitch is stressed in various ways starting from the shapes, the colours, the animations and the graphical elements. In order to strengthen the link with Facebook, we used some graphical elements that could be associated with the platform, such as the chat that explains the research at the base of the project.
