B2C WEBSITE

Bellini Yacht

Bellini Yacht commissioned Mònade to design and develop a website that reflects their core values and allows for easy content updates. The design features spacious layouts crafted to embody the luxury and precision of Bellini Yacht, ensuring an intuitive user experience. In my role, I autonomously designed the UI and visual language, collaborating closely with the design lead for feedback. Working closely with developers, we integrated efficient content management functionalities, empowering the client to maintain their digital presence independently. This collaborative effort resulted in a website that enhances user engagement and effectively communicates Bellini Yacht core values.

Bellini Yacht is a part of Bellini Group, a nautical company that has been active since 1960, specializes in various aspects of the nautical industry. Bellini Yacht contacted Mònade to create a website that would serve as the primary touchpoint with its customers.

GOAL

To develop a website that could effectively communicate the brand's values while allowing independently content update.

Design and developement

The project began with a kick-off meeting to understand the client's needs and vision. Bellini Yacht is built on several key values that needed to be visually represented on the website:

  1. Iconic: it should be memorable and recognizable
  2. Minimalism and Exclusivity: distinctive and refined product, speaking for themselves without the need for ostentation
  3. Union of Design and Nature: seamless fusion of enclosed and open spaces
After a visual research and competitor analysis, we defined the structure and organization of the site to be clear and intuitive. Given the tight deadline, I selected a visual language that met the client's needs while ensuring a straightforward execution.
The interface design aimed to embody the brand's values featuring a clean layout with generous white space; while curved lines were employed to seamlessly integrate design elements with natural references,symbolizing their union.
Throughout the design process, I prioritized understanding the capabilities and constraints of our development technology. This ensured the client's ability to effortlessly update content while ensuring an high level of personalization in the design element like hover effects on buttons to elevate usability and engagement

[A] I aligned the website with the client's social media campaign by incorporating a blur effect on unreleased yacht images, ensuring consistency across their communication strategy

OUTCOME

“Bellini Yacht” is a website that underlines the brand values providing an elegant and intuitive user experience. It allows the client to independently update content, ensuring the site remains dynamic and relevant. However, after the project delivery, additional modifications were requested which I did not oversee. Therefore, the final outcome presents some differences from the design and development we initial delivered.

RESULT

The website meets the practical needs of content management and user interaction, ensuring that the client can maintain and update the site with ease. This comprehensive approach has produced a digital platform that effectively communicates the brand's values and supports the client's business goals.
