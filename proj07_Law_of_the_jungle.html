Law of the Jungle

Instagram proposes itself as an open-minded platform, but through the use of policies about sexual content, it denies its users’ freedom of expression, and it reinforces the taboos of our society. Law of the Jungle is a game structured on four levels; each of them refers to a particular section of the Instagram nudity policy that in some statements is discriminatory and ambiguous, especially regarding the female body. The main elements of Instagram identity are reworked to create a new language that could be easily associated with the social media. The project bases on a strong provocation: what happens if this policy will be applied to the entire animal world. The users are called to work as human moderator censoring nudity contents, of both animals and humans, intending to make them reflect on which vision of the society we have according to the policy. [C] At the end of the game, the users receive a profile based on their total score, which ironically identifies their moderator’s abilities in the entire animal world

[D] The gallery contains all the images (and the corresponding explanations) that Instagram would censor
