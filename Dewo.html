Dewo

Fostering children’s autonomy by making daily chores engaging

Dewo is an IoT box designed for children aged 6–8, turning everyday chores into engaging and interactive experiences. Built-in sensors provide visual and sound feedback, making interactions clear and engaging, while the companion app allows parents to assign tasks and track progress. Children engage with playful characters, creating a seamless and enjoyable routine that fosters autonomy and responsibility.

During a child’s day, there are a lot of important activities, which are often perceived as annoying, even if they are essential for their growth, such as tidying up. On the other hand, parents want their kids to be more autonomous.

CHALLENGE

We aimed to design an IoT device that makes these tasks engaging and motivating for children, while allowing parents to support their autonomy in a seamless and intuitive way. The solution needed to balance playfulness with functionality, encouraging independence without constant parental intervention, and fostering a positive daily routine for both children and parents.

We explored children’s activity preferences through desk research and hands-on sessions with kids aged 6–8, complemented by interviews with parents. We found that obligatory tasks are often perceived as boring, which highlighted the need to transform a mundane activity into a playful and engaging experience.

[A] Some of the cards we designed for the hands-on activities with children

From these insights, we identified “tidy up” as a key focus and developed an animal-shaped box, associating tidying with caring for a pet. This concept guided the definition of system components, UX scenarios, and an experience map for the application.

SOLUTION

Dewo is a smart box that turns tidying up into an engaging activity. Built-in sensors trigger visual and sound responses when children insert objects, making the interaction dynamic and motivating.

[G] Thanks to the weight sensor, Dewo perceives how many objects are inserted reacting every time by changing its eyes and saying short sentences or words that remind of food

The companion app complements this experience with a child-friendly reward system: each completed task earns points that can be used to unlock and customize characters, encouraging participation and fostering autonomy.

RESULT

Dewo's interactive design turns mundane chores into engaging experiences, fostering independence and responsibility. The project shows how technology can enhance learning processes and promote a smoother daily routine for families.
