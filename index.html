Anna Gazza

UX/UI designer and information designer

Hello there! I’m Anna, a designer passionate about information and data visualization design, UX/UI design, editorial, interaction design, and web design. I think designing is having responsibility, so I consider the ethics of what I am doing and the implications it could have. That’s because I believe Design has an active role in bringing changes in different aspects of life, both on a private and a societal level.

I consider myself a curious, motivated and collaborative person. My curiosity has always pushed me to do in-depth research on the topics I face to figure out the best solutions I can. Thanks to team-working I’ve learned the importance of looking at the same thing from different points of view to reach a more complete result.

I am currently based in Brescia (Italy), but I am looking for new opportunities and challenges to test myself, improve my skills and learn new ones. I constantly look at new things to do and new ways to grow, but there is a thing that will never change: I am an extremely clumsy person who usually pet animals.
