Integrated report of Museo Egizio

The Egyptian Museum of Turin is a museum specialized in Egyptian archaeology and anthropology. The institution commissioned to Accurat the design of their first integrated report. The aim was to create a document able to merge quantitative and qualitative aspects of the museum’s activities focusing on the elements that determine its value. The report gives a comprehensive view of the institution’s activities, from the research to the social inclusion, narrated through impactful and easy to read visualizations. For this reason, the legend is often contained in the infographic itself. Starting from the museum’s visual identity, the report was divided into 4 sections, each of them characterized by a specific colour of the brand identity. Website
