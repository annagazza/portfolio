EXPERIMENTAL GAME

Law of the Jungle

"Law of the Jungle" is a four-level digital game examining Instagram's nudity policy, highlighting its discriminatory and ambiguous aspects, particularly against the female body. Players act as moderators censoring nudity content of both animals and humans, prompting a reflection on which vision of the society we have according to the policy. The project aims to encourage a conversation about the implications of such policies on freedom of expression and gender equality. I contributed to all phases, focusing on designing the first level, and designing and coding the gallery.

Building on our research from "Making the Invisible Visible," we developed a digital artifact highlighting our key findings on Instagram's censorship of the human body.

Despite presenting itself as an open-minded platform, Instagram's nudity policies reveal biases, restrict freedom of expression, and reinforce social taboos. The policy guidelines are often ambiguous, resulting in image removals or account blocks that leave users uncertain about what is acceptable.

GOAL

To turn research findings based on data into a communication design artifact for the publics.

The analysis of Instagram's nudity policy revealed its ambiguity and lack of clarity: it is either too vague to be fully understood or so specific that it becomes discriminatory.
From these insights, we sought a visual artefact to convey them and eventually chose an ironic and provocative game. This tone stemmed from a reflection: What would happen if Instagram's nudity policy applied to the entire animal world?

This question led to two parallel phases: defining a user experience that provides all necessary elements for critical reflection and researching the animal world to collect useful elements for the experience.

[A] Information architecture

We created a visual language to emphasize the project's provocative and critical nature. By analyzing Instagram's brand identity, we developed new language that could be easily associated with the platform while still distinct from it. This ensures users feel part of the Instagram community while engaging with the project's critique.

OUTCOME

The final experience is a game consisting of various quizzes where the juxtaposition of ambiguous images helps Instagram users reflect on the community guidelines. After an introductory part explaining the topic, users read the Instagram policy on sexual content. This policy is gradually revealed throughout the game, which consists of eight tasks, two for each theme in the guidelines, with a series of images to censor.

[B] At the end of each task the users can read the explanation of each image to understand why it would be censored or not

[C] I designed this quiz where users choose between two alternatives that follow Instagram's policies. I mimicked Instagram's photo editing interface to create a familiar experience

[D] At the end of the game, the users receive a profile based on their total score, which ironically identifies their moderator’s abilities in the entire animal world

The game concludes with our reflections on the policy and a gallery of censored images I designed and coded.

[E] The gallery offers two views: one for detailed explanations of censored images and another for an overview of censored images grouped in categories

RESULT

"Law of the Jungle" highlighted the Instagram policy inconsistencies and biases, encouraging users to critically reflect on the societal norms and standards imposed by such regulations.
