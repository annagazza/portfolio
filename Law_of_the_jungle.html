EXPERIMENTAL GAME

Law of the Jungle

An interactive exploration of visual content censorship

"Law of the Jungle" is a four-level digital game exploring Instagram’s nudity policy and its discriminatory ambiguities, particularly regarding the female body. Players act as moderators, censoring nudity content of animals and humans, prompting reflection on the societal norms embedded in the policy. The project encourages conversation about freedom of expression and gender equality.

Despite presenting itself as an open-minded platform, Instagram's nudity policies reveal biases, restrict freedom of expression, and reinforce social taboos. The policy guidelines are often ambiguous, resulting in image removals or account blocks that leave users uncertain about what is acceptable.

CHALLENGE

Research findings revealed subtle discrimination and ambiguities in Instagram’s nudity policies, making it difficult for users to interpret what is acceptable and understand the social and cultural implications of content moderation. The challenge was to translate this research into an engaging experience that encourages users to reflect on the social and cultural implications of content moderation.

Analyzing Instagram's nudity policy revealed its ambiguity: it is either too vague to understand or so specific that it becomes discriminatory.
Based on these reflections, we hypothesized various digital artifacts, ultimately settling on a provocative quiz game inspired by a critical question: What would happen if Instagram’s nudity policy applied to the entire animal world?

[A] Information architecture

To support this critical and provocative approach, we reworked Instagram’s visual language to create a new one that felt familiar yet distinct. This ensured users could engage with the game and its critique while still feeling part of the Instagram community.

SOLUTION

The final experience is a quiz game where users play as human moderators, engaging with ambiguous images to reflect on Instagram’s community guidelines.
After a brief introduction that sets the stage, the policy on sexual content is gradually revealed as users progress through the tasks. This gradual reveal guides users step by step, providing the necessary context to decide which images to censor while structuring the experience around the actual sections of the policy.
We designed the game to reflect key elements of Instagram’s brand identity, creating an intuitive experience that feels familiar yet encourages critical reflection. Each task includes explanations for the images, helping users understand why a particular image would be censored or not.

[B] At the end of each task the users can read the explanation of each image to understand why it would be censored or not

[C] I designed this quiz where users choose between two alternatives that follow Instagram's policies. I mimicked Instagram's photo editing interface to create a familiar experience

[D] At the end of the game, the users receive a profile based on their total score, which ironically identifies their moderator’s abilities in the entire animal world

The game concludes with our reflections on the policy sections and a gallery of censored images.

[E] The gallery offers two views: one for detailed explanations of censored images and another for an overview of censored images grouped in categories

RESULT

Law of the Jungle highlighted the Instagram policy inconsistencies and biases, encouraging users to critically reflect on the societal norms and standards imposed by such regulations. Through its interactive design and familiar visual language, we made this reflection engaging, accessible, and impactful, guiding users to explore the implications of content moderation in a playful yet critical way.
